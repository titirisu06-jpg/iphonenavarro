# Video Marketing Strategy — Social Media

Guidelines for creating high-impact promotional videos for social media.

## 1. The Hook (0-3s)
- **Goal**: Stop the scroll.
- **Action**: Use a bold statement or a high-energy visual.
- **Example**: "El iPhone que buscás, al precio que no esperás."

## 2. Social Proof (3-6s)
- **Goal**: Build trust instantly.
- **Action**: Mention sales volume, years in business, or ratings.
- **Example**: "+10.000 iPhones entregados en todo el país."

## 3. High-Key Aesthetics
- **Goal**: Professionalism and brightness.
- **Action**: Use white (#FFFFFF) backgrounds with high contrast text.
- **Benefit**: Feels premium, clean, and "Apple-style".

## 4. Benefit Hierarchy
- **Primary**: Price / Value (Tomamos tu usado).
- **Secondary**: Convenience (Envíos a todo el país).
- **Tertiary**: Security (Garantía oficial).

## 5. Direct Call to Action (CTA)
- **Goal**: Immediate conversion.
- **Action**: Huge brand URL and clear WhatsApp icon.
- **Timing**: At least 3 seconds of screen time at the end.

## 6. Technical Excellence (UX Pro Max)
- **Typography**: Bold, sans-serif, tight tracking.
- **Transitions**: Scale and fade (zoom-in/out) for energy.
- **Shadows**: Ultra-soft, multi-layered shadows for 3D depth.
